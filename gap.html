Understanding the gap

The Cambridge Public School District has a 25-35% gap in performance between low-income and high-income students. Similar gaps exist between white students and people-of-color. Performance in the absolute for low-income demographics has been steadily raising at nearly 1% per year. If this continues, we will be at 100% proficiency by around 2080.
